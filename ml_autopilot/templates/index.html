About Us

Welcome to ML on AutoPilot, your go-to platform for hassle-free machine learning model creation and deployment. Our mission is to empower users with the ability to effortlessly harness the potential of machine learning for their datasets to enhance decision making.


What We Do

At ML on AutoPilot, we simplify the entire machine learning process. Whether you're a seasoned data scientist or a newcomer to the world of AI, our platform is designed to guide you through every step of the journey.

  1. Dataset Analysis:br Upload your dataset, and we provide you with an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) report, offering valuable insights into your data.

  2. Data Preprocessing: If your data needs cleaning, we guide you through the process, ensuring your dataset is in optimal condition for modeling

  3. Handling Categorical Variables:
    We assist in managing categorical variables through methods like one-hot encoding or label encoding, enhancing the effectiveness of your model.

  4. Model Selection: Choose between our two modes: Manual and Automated.
    • Manual: Specify your preferences, including regression or classification, and input additional parameters like the value of k for k-NN.
    • Automated:Let us handle the entire process, from algorithm selection to parameter tuning.

Your Toolkit

Upon completion of the processing and training steps, we provide you with a personalized toolkit:

  • Pickle File: Download your trained model in a convenient pickle file format for seamless integration into your projects.

  • JSON File: Access a JSON file containing information about the encoding methods applied during preprocessing.

  • Handbook [Text File]: Obtain a detailed text file helping you in using the trained model and documentation of the algorithm used, ensuring transparency in your model's methodology.

  • IPython Notebook (ipynb): For those who love to visualize, explore, and experiment, we provide an IPython notebook with Gradio interface, allowing you to interact with your model effortlessly.
